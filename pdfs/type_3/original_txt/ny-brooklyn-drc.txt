BAPTISMS
There are three distinct lists of Baptisms. The first, extend-
ing from 1660 to 17 10, had been entered in the body of the
book. The second consists of a few detached leaves and runs
from August 25, 1676, to October 20, 1678. The third, consist-
ing of two loose leaves, commences with October 7, 1688, and
ends with August 9, 1696, while the years 1691, 1692, and 1693
are missing. The second and third list may be fragments of
another church Record.
